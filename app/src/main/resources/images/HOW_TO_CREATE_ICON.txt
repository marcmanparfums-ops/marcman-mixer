  CUM SA CREEZI ICONITA - 3 METODE
==========================================

METODA 1: ONLINE (CEL MAI SIMPLU)
----------------------------------
1. Mergi pe: https://www.canva.com/create/icons/
   sau: https://www.flaticon.com/
   
2. Cauta: "perfume bottle", "lab equipment", "M letter"

3. Descarca varianta PNG (512x512) si converteste-o in ICO:
   - https://cloudconvert.com/png-to-ico
   - https://convertico.com/

4. Salveaza fisierul final ca "mixer.ico"

5. Copiaza in: app/src/main/resources/images/mixer.ico
